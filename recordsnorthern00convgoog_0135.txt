8 Reconns or rurs

Martis quintum vizt diem mensis Maii proxime futurum
vel alium diem citra, si opus fuerit, istis horis et loco,
reservavit. Necnon convocationem sive Synodum
provincialem proximam usque ad et in eundem diem seu
aliquem diem citra, si oporteat, istis horis ct loco,
continuavit et prorogavit. Et monuit omnes et singulos
nunc interessentes adhuc rursus comparendum, ad
ulterius procedendum in dicta Convocntione sive Synodo,
prout natura et qualitas ejusdem de se exigunt et
requirunt, prout patet ex tenore schedulae inde per
dominum Reverendissimum patrem lectae.

This Convocation, April, 1640, coincided with “The
Short Parliament‚” summoned in consequence oi the
rupture between England and Scotland‚ an outbreak
known as “the first Bishops' War.”
